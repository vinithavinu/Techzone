DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

Department Of BCA :

Name : Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA)

Eligibility : Scoring 50% in 10+2 Exam (45% for SC/ST) and Pass in Mathematics/Business Mathematics and English at 10+2

Duration : 3 years (Semester System)

Certificate Awarding Body : Madras University

Year of starting : 2019

Number of Teachers : 6

BCA Course Overview :

The BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) course was introduced in the July 2019 academic session. It is a 3-year degree program in computer applications, affiliated with Madras University. The course follows a semester system and admissions usually take place in May/June each year. You can find admission details on our Avichi College official website.

Objective :

The primary goal of the BCA program is to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to become proficient Information Technology graduates. Upon completion, BCA graduates can pursue careers as Programmers, System Analysts, Administrators, and more.

Recognition & Intake :

Designed, prepared, and approved by Madras University, the BCA course is a reputable 3-year degree program that confers a degree awarded by the university itself. The college offers 50 seats for each academic session, ensuring a limited and focused learning environment.
