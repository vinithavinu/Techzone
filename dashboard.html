AVICHI COLLEGE OF ARTS AND SCIENCE

AVICHI COLLEGE INFO:

  • Avichi College of Arts and Science, a prestigious institution under the AVM Rajeswari Educational Trust, was established by Smt. AVM. Rajeswari and other esteemed Trustees. This renowned Trust also manages Avichi Higher Secondary Schools for boys and girls, as well as the AVM Rajeswari Matriculation Higher Secondary School. The Trust's commitment to education led to the establishment of the Avichi College of Arts and Science to cater to the growing demand for higher education in the Chennai region.
  • The Avichi College of Arts and Science provides a range of undergraduate courses under the semester pattern with the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS). These include B.Com (General, Accounting and Finance, Bank Management, and Corporate Secretaryship), B.Sc (Visual Communication and Computer Science), B.C.A (Computer Application), B.B.A (Business Administration), and BA (Criminology and Police Administration).
  • To enhance students' overall development, the college has various cells and clubs, such as the Entrepreneurial Development Cell, Student Council, NSS, YRC, RRC, as well as specialized classes in sports, singing, dance, Korean, Bengali, Hindi, and French. With its commitment to quality education and holistic growth, Avichi College of Arts and Science continues to be a preferred choice for students in Chennai and beyond.
