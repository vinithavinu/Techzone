SPORTS

" SILAMBAM "

Indira, a talented Silambam player, achieved remarkable feats. She won a gold medal in the International Youth Games Championship in Pokhara, Nepal (12/07/2023). She also set a Star World Book of Records by rotating a stick continuously for 5 hours (05/11/2023). In the following year, she secured gold medals at the National Youth Games in Delhi (01/02/2024) and the State-level Silambam Championship in Theni (25/02/2024) in double stick events.
